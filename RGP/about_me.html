The computer
is my life

I love my computer! Not because he knows how to play powerful games, but because he gives me the opportunity to take a break from the hustle and bustle of the day. I can spend hours on end enthusiastically writing code, gradually watching a website page appear out of nowhere. It's just fascinating.

Relaxing at
the computer

A lot of mistakes, edits, anxious waiting and minutes of nervous tension... In a short period of time (it seems so when you are carried away) that you spent at the monitor screen, you manage to experience a lot of emotions.

The End
Result

And what a feeling of euphoria you feel, realizing that all this was not in vain, that I got exactly what I imagined when I sat down in front of the monitor, that I was so stubbornly going to this result, not noticing how it was getting dark outside the window, the air warmed by the warmth of the day is replaced by the coolness of the night, twitter early birds and sunrise...

A new day

He comes suddenly... And I understand what fatigue has fallen on me for a sleepless night, but fatigue is pleasant, and there are new tasks ahead, a new struggle with myself for a new result that will suit me, for new heights!

And again
evening

I brew a cup of fragrant tea, sit comfortably at the monitor... The first sip... New page, new code... A new test...

Conclusion

I'm still a beginner in the field of web development, but I can say with confidence - I like it! As a person with a technical mindset, I believe that this is what I have been missing for many years.
